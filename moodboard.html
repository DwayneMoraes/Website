Projects

Moodboard

Assignment

Create a personal mood board that captures the elements that shape you as an artist. Gather images, colors, textures, words, and other visuals that reflect your style, influences, and creative interests. The goal is to clearly express your artistic identity and the direction you want your work to grow.

Artwork Description

This mood board brings together the art, music, films, and ideas that shape my creative identity. Its mix of quotes, imagery, and cultural references reflects the blend of humor, reflection, and inspiration that drives my work.

Prompt Swamp

Assignment

Create an original artwork in Adobe Illustrator using a prompt written by one of your classmates. Instead of developing your own concept, you will interpret someone else’s idea and translate it into a visual design. The goal is to explore how different artists respond to the same source of inspiration and to practice adapting your creative process to outside direction.

Artwork Description

A classmate’s prompt inspired this Illustrator piece of a bird wrapped in a slice of bread. Simple shapes and grainy texture give the character a light, playful feel.

Remix Culture

Assignment

Make a digital artwork by remixing existing images. Combine and transform them to create a new visual idea that’s different from the originals.

Artwork Description

I combined a portrait, flowers, and a dramatic sky to create a new image that blends natural beauty with a surreal mood. By remixing these elements, I transformed unrelated visuals into a single, cohesive scene.

Pixel Art

Assignment

Create a small pixel-based artwork using a limited canvas and color palette. The goal is to understand the roots of early computer graphics and practice building an image one pixel at a time, focusing on shape, clarity, and visual simplicity.

Arrtwork Description

I created a bright, playful pixel fish using bold colors and simple shapes. By working at a low resolution, I focused on capturing personality through minimal details and repeating smaller fish to build a lively underwater scene.

Vector Intro

Assignment

Create a clean vector-style graphic using Photoshop’s shape tools and pen tool. The goal is to practice building smooth, scalable forms and designing a simple logo or icon that reads clearly at any size.

Artwork Description

I designed a playful dog logo using vector shapes in Photoshop, focusing on clean curves and a simple, expressive face. The warm gradient background and bold silhouette help the design feel friendly and inviting, fitting the “Pup Paradise” theme.
